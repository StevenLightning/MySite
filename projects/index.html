My Interests

Firstly, I love nature—trees, mountains, rivers, etc. I want to live somewhere that's very green with lots of trees and some sort of body of water bordering my property or at least nearby. I also love hiking and exploring in the mountains. It's always really cool to discover a small cave or some other interesting landmark while doing so. Honestly, I don't spend as much time out in nature as I would like, though last semester I managed to go hiking almost every week due to a goal that I set for my health class. I would like to continue hiking often. I enjoy rock climbing as well, which is another thing I would like to do more often.

I love fantasy and science fiction. The more creative the world, universe, or concept the better. The book Storm Thief by Chris Wooding is a good example of this. It takes place on a post-apocalyptic city-covered plateau island which is beset by storms that can alter anything they touch, doing anything from simply rearranging things to creating unusual monsters and many other things. When I was in elementary school I used to read a lot, but unfortunately that lessened as I got older. In the last couple years, however, I've been listening to audiobooks as I can do so while driving or working. I've gone through The Wheel of Time and The Stormlight Archive series that way, as well as a few other books. I love being able to get my fill of the stories from these books without needing to take much extra time to do so. Movies can be really great for their stories as well. Some of the movies that I love include The Lord of the Rings, the Miyazaki/Studio Ghibli films, Star Wars, etc.

Lastly, and perhaps most importantly, I'd like to talk about some of my creative endeavors. I've always had a drive to create new things that are interesting, exciting, and different from anything else. When I was I was younger I loved creating my ideas with Legos, and I also really enjoyed taking things apart and building them into something new. I've always been pretty good with computers and I use that as a creative medium as well. I enjoy using programming to bring some of my ideas to life. I also enjoy 3d modeling. I've spent a good amount of my free time this year teaching myself how to use the Unity game engine and create my own games. I've also played around some with video editing recently. My plan for the future is to first get a job programming after school and then, provided I can get the traction I need, to eventually be entirely self-employed creating games and possibly YouTube videos.
